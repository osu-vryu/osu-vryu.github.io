VRyu

An immersive VR experience teaching newcomers the traditional Japanese sword arts through fun, gamified exercises

problem statement

Getting into the sword arts typically requires a large investment in blades or bokken. VRyu exists to give the broader world a peek into Kenjutsu as a martial art, and the culture that surrounds it.
