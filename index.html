VRyu

An immersive VR experience teaching newcomers the traditional Japanese sword arts through fun, gamified exercises

problem statement

Getting into the sword arts typically requires a large investment in blades or bokken. VRyu exists to give the broader world a peek into kenjutsu as a martial art, and the culture that surrounds it.

slicing

The player is presented with a variety of slicing challenges, testing both their timing and precision. This includes cutting moving targets, like the watermelons pictured above.

mannequin enemy

In the final game, the player's ultimate challenge will be attacking and dodging a humanoid enemy styled after a mannequin, seen above. This simulates the act of sparring with a training partner, an essential practice for training key swordsmanship skills.

the dojo

The game takes place in a realistic dojo scene, the type that one would actually train for kenjutsu in!

the tea room

The game ends in a lovingly crafted tea room, dispelling the myth that sword arts are about violence by showing the more peaceful about disciplined core of their related philosophies.
